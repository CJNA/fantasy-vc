## The realization that reframes everything

She has a post titled **"a clever way to build a VC track record without a VC job."** Her method,
verbatim thesis: build a **fantasy portfolio publicly** — treat it like a real fund with
constraints (fund size, check size, stage, ownership), track your decisions, share the thinking
(her example: Turner Novak). A second post, **"how to build a track record as a junior VC without
writing checks,"** says the same: *track a hypothetical portfolio before you're officially writing
checks.*

**She is already telling 88,000 aspiring VCs to do, by hand in a spreadsheet, exactly what Fantasy
VC is.** We are the product version of her most-repeated advice. That's the positioning, the
channel, and the wedge in one.

## The plan — fun first

### P0 · Fun & retention (small, ship now)
1. **Streaks as a hero feature.** Daily streak counter on the seed screen and endcard; "🔥 N-day
   streak," "3-win streak vs the AI scouts." *Why: her #1 predictor of who makes it (consistency)
   doubles as the retention hook. (#3, #34)* — small; already scoped in FUN-FIRST P1, promote it.
2. **"Defined by your best pick" endcard beat.** One line naming your single highest-multiple
   position + what it did for the fund. *Why: power-law is how VCs actually think about their
   careers (#27); it's a more emotional payoff than total P&L.* — small.
3. **Shareable result reads as a credential, not a flex.** The challenge/share card leads with the
   record (boards played · first-attempt calibration · times beat the field), not one 23× score.
   *Why: matches how she says juniors get noticed — consistent evidence, not a lucky hit (#11).* — small.

### P1 · The credential layer (the thing her audience will pay attention to)
4. **Capture the *why* — a one-line thesis per pick.** Optional memo field at seed; surface it on
   the endcard next to the outcome ("you said 'clean gov, real moat' → 89×"). *Why: case studies
   test reasoning, not the answer (#28). This is the single biggest gap between "a game" and "a
   track record a firm respects."* — medium. This is also the Analyst-tier / paywall seam.
5. **A real record page.** Persistent history keyed to the player: boards played, streak,
   first-attempt calibration trend, rocket hit-rate, fraud dodge-rate. *Why: this is the automated
   version of the spreadsheet she tells everyone to keep (#82); it's the exportable "self-pitch
   deck" for the AI era (#3's self-pitch-deck story, #64 AI angle).* — medium; needs the collector
   (already live) or localStorage-first.
6. **Map factors to her real red-flag vocabulary.** Rename/re-skin toward founding timeline,
   leadership stability, capital efficiency, revenue trajectory, burn — and add a burn-vs-revenue
   fraud tell. *Why: fidelity is the moat (Ryan's fantasy-baseball bar + her concrete eval
   framework, #93). Makes a real VC nod instead of wince.* — medium; a balancing task.

### P2 · Position & distribute (once P0/P1 land)
7. **"The VC case-study gym."** Frame the AI scouts + VC-Bench as sparring partners for the exact
   skill firms test. *Why: she explicitly tells people to use AI to practice case studies (#64);
   we already built the AI (#3 booked-scores).* — positioning + copy, not code.
8. **Newsletter-native distribution.** Her audience is the whole TAM. A "challenge the VC
   Demystified board" angle, or a guest board/collab, is the channel. *Why: recognition before
   access (#34); she already recommends the behavior we enable.* — partnership, not code.

## What NOT to build (guard the fun)
- Don't turn the casual endcard into a memo form. The *why*-capture (P1 #4) stays **optional** and
  the quant/credential layer stays behind the 🔬 Analyst toggle — casual players still see
  multiple / profit / risk / verdict. (FUN-FIRST discipline: fun is free, credential is opt-in.)
- Don't over-fit factors to one person's framework; her red flags are a *sanity check* on fidelity,
  not a spec to copy literally.
- Streaks must never punish missing a day into a shame spiral — celebrate return, don't guilt.

## Talking to Nicole (earned hooks, from her own posts)
